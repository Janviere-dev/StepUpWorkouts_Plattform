Emmanuel's Story

Emmanuel was known for his kindness, his quiet laugh, and his unwavering faith. But deep down, he carried a quiet insecurity—he felt small. Not just in size, but in presence. His reflection in the mirror never quite matched the resilience he felt inside.

One day, while helping his uncle lift bags of cement at a family site, Emmanuel’s arms buckled. His uncle looked at him and said with a chuckle, “These bones need some protein!” Everyone laughed. Emmanuel did too. But later that night, he stood alone under the stars and whispered:

“I want to feel strong. Not to impress, but to carry the future I see in me.”
